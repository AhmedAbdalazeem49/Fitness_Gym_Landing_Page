More Than Fitness

To keep the body in good health is a duty, otherwise we shall not be able to keep our mind strong and clear. When it comes to eating right and exercising, there is no'I'll start tomorrow'. It never gets easier, you just get better. The gym is not the social club for the fit. Exercise not only changes your body, it changes your mind, your attitude and your mood. Exercise should be regarded as a tribute to the heart. Good things come to those who sweat. Of all the paths you take in life, make sure a few of them are dirt.
